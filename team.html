Meet the Team

Clinical Staff

Aric Murphy DDS, MD

Surgeon

Dr. Aric Murphy and his family have lived in Fort Collins since 2012. They chose the area because of its small town feel, great schools, college sports and access to the Rocky Mountains. Dr. Murphy completed Medical School and Residency from Mayo Clinic in Rochester Minnesota and an additional year of Fellowship Training in Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgery treating children under 12 years of age. He has been on a number of surgical mission trips, including Guatemala and Vietnam, correcting cleft lip and palate deformities. He loves taking his three teenagers skiing, kayaking, backpacking and seasonal trips to local orchards picking fresh apples and peaches.

Jackie

Clinical Manager

Originally from Parker, Colorado, Jackie moved to Fort Collins to attend FRCC and completed the Dental Assisting program in 2012. Dr. Murphy saw a diamond in the making, quickly snatched her up and she's been here since. She adds an incredibly calm, compassionate, warm personality to our group as she competently juggles the clinical demands. You can find Jackie and her husband outdoors together every chance they get enjoying activities such as disc golf, hiking, hunting, fishing, and golfing.

Rose

Surgical Assistant

Rose is such a valuable part of our team, sharing her genuinely sweet, fun, caring attitude with our patients. We were thrilled to keep her on after her internship through the FRCC Dental Assisting program as she discovered a love of oral surgery. She enjoys reading historical romance, oil painting, knitting, rock concerts, and spending time with her Corgi-Havanese mix named Rouples and adorable ducks, affectionately named Aric, Allan, and Murphy.

Jess

Surgical Assistant

Our friendly, intelligent, sweet Jess loves translating for our Spanish speaking patients. The relief she sees is personally fulfilling and plays a big role in her decision to work as a healthcare professional. Currently studying for her masters in Toxicology at CSU, she enjoys working at her family's restaurant with her twin brother and playing at the family farm where she braids her horses tail and mane. Spare moments are spent horseback riding, painting, and lifting weights.

Jeanette

Surgical Assistant

Jeanette hails from St. Petersburg, FL, she, plus husband traded the east coast for the Colorado Rocky Mountains in 2017 and she enjoys skiing, hiking, and climbing. The FRCC Dental Assisting program encouraged her ability to communicate well with the patients and meet their individual needs. You might see her around town performing improv with her local comedy troupe, Scam Likely, singing karaoke, or walking her dog, Pocky. She most appreciates Dr. Murphy's patience and willingness to educate.

Administrative Staff

Jennifer

Office Manager

A native Coloradoan, Jennifer grew up in Lakewood then graduated from CSU where she was also a collegiate swimmer. Before joining Murphy OMS she spent 10 years in Women's Health as an educator and doula. Her passion is helping patients feel well informed and confident through every step of their experience here. She loves spending time with her five kids and five fun, crazy grandkids. In her free time, you'll find her traveling, reading, or at the Pilates studio.

Melissa
