Murphy Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

Murphy Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery offers a wide variety of specialized services focused on curing problems relating to jaw, teeth and other maxillofacial issues. We provide comprehensive surgical services for adults and children, and are committed to delivering the highest quality and most technologically advanced OMS services. Please call our office at (970) 420-6848 and schedule your appointment with Aric Murphy DDS, MD today.

Led by Aric Murphy, MD, DDS, our caring and compassionate staff offer a wide range of inpatient and outpatient cosmetic and functional surgical services catered to patients of all ages. Our goal is to provide our patients with the most convenient and personalized care, while delivering results that are as cosmetically appealing as they are functionally healthy.

  • In-network with a number of dental insurances
  • We fee match most out-of-network dental insurances
  • We accept Medicaid (under 21 years of age) and CPH+
  • We offer a 15% discount for anyone without dental insurance

Aric Murphy MD, DDS

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon
Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgeon

Dr. Aric Murphy DDS, MD is an Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon, with Fellowship Training in Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgery.

Following his residency training and obtaining his Medical Degree from the Mayo Clinic, Dr. Murphy did an additional year of Fellowship Training in Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgery for additional experience in treating children 12 years old and younger with oral surgery needs. He has been on surgical mission trips including traveling to Guatemala and Vietnam correcting children with cleft lip and palate deformities and other facial disorders. He and his family have lived in Fort Collins since 2012.

Education:

  • Fellowship: Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgery, University of Oklahoma
  • Medical Degree and Residency: Mayo Clinic, Rochester Minnesota
  • Doctor of Dental Surgery: University of Indiana, Summa Cum Laude
  • Undergraduate Studies: Kansas State University
