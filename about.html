About Us

Murphy Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery offers a wide variety of specialized services focused on curing problems relating to jaw, teeth and other maxillofacial problems. We provide comprehensive surgical services for adults and children, and are committed to delivering the highest quality and most technologically advanced OMS services. Please call our office at (970) 420-6848 and schedule your appointment with Aric Murphy DDS, MD today.

Aric Murphy MD, DDS

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon
Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgeon

Dr. Aric Murphy DDS, MD is an Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon, with Fellowship Training in Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgery.

Following his residency training & obtaining his Medical Degree from the Mayo Clinic, Dr. Murphy did an additional year of Fellowship Training in Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgery for additional experience in treating children 12 years old and younger with oral surgery needs. He has been on surgical mission trips including traveling to Guatemala & Vietnam correcting children with cleft lip & palate deformities and other facial disorders.
